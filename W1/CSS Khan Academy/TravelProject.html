Travel to Sri Lanka

The most famous sights of Sri Lanka

  • Sigiriya

At the top of the cliff, which rises above the surrounding plain at 170 meters, in the V century the residence of King Kassapa was built. In its gardens were one of the first fountains in the world, which operate today.

  • Adam's Peak

A Lankan proverb says: “Whoever was not at the peak of Adam is a fool. Who was there twice is a fool too.” The height of the mountain is 2,243 meters, in order to meet the dawn on the summit (precisely for it, many travelers decide on this adventure), they will have to go on a journey at night.
The rise takes 3-4 hours, depending on physical fitness, you will walk the stairs with a railing, which is illuminated only in the season. To the observation deck - 5000 steps.

  • Yala National Park

Once there were hunting grounds for wealthy British colonists, and today there is a protected area of about 130,000 hectares. The “nail of the program” is a population of unique Ceylon leopards (about 30 individuals) that are endangered. In addition to them, elephants, deer, wild boars, buffaloes, crocodiles and wild birds live here.
